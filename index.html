Introduction

Welcome to the Sekai! These places called “Sekais” are located in the fictional world of “Hatsune Miku: Colorful Stage.”

Each Sekai is created by a group’s emotions. The strongest emotion shapes the world and chooses the leader.These places called “Sekai’s” are located in the fictional world of “Hatsume Miku: Colorful Stage”. This document will outline all the different Sekai’s and what they all need/have in each of them. Each individual Sekai is created by a group of peoples feelings, with one member of the groups feeling’s strongest making them the leader of said group. These feelings help shape and create the Sekais in each groups unique path.

Despite differing musical styles, all Sekais share common mechanics and elements.

Units

Each Sekai is made up of four different people and each of their respective goals and dreams is what make the Sekai appear in the first place. The Unit takes on the form of whichever type of music the group wants to perform. Along with the four members there are also people known as Vitural Singers in each Sekai. They only exsit in the Sekai and are there to help each unit grow more and more through music.

The groups reflect different genres: rock, idol pop, rap, musical theater, and melancholic ballads.

Types

With this everything in each Sekai has an “attribute” that is connected to it. The items in question are the decorations and the plants, but we will get into more of that later. The types of attributes include:

  • Happy
  • Pure
  • Cute
  • Cool
  • Mysterious

Each attribute is connected to one plant and one decoration element. These are also connected with each character of the Sekai and help boost their skills when they are applied during shows.

Leo Need

Leo/Need is the first Unit that we see here and they are the the Band/Rock group. They are a group of high school friends who at first fought with each other, but thanks to Miku, they were able to reconnect.With the help of Senior Miku and Luka, they have grown to purse the dream of going pro.

Members: Ichika, Shiho, Honami, and Saki. Vocaloids: Miku, Luka.

let genre = "Rock";

MORE MORE JUMP

MORE MORE JUMP is an Idol group that is made up of three girls who had once given up and one girl who wants to be an Idol more than anything. Their music is very J-Pop style but also have a few songs that help expand their range. With the help of Idol Miku and Rin, they have their dream of performing at the Dome.

Members: Minori, Haruka, Airi, and Shizuku. Vocaloids: Miku, Rin.

let genre = "Idol Pop";

Vivid Bad Squad

Vivid Bad Squad is a Street artist group who’s main focus is Rap style music. They all united to try and surpass a legendary event that happened many years ago. With the help of Vivid Bad Squad Miku, MEIKO, and Len, they have their dream of surppasing the legendary event, “RAD Weekend”.

Members: Kohane, Akito, An, and Toya. Vocaloids: Miku, Meiko, Len.

let genre = "Rap";

Wonderland x Showtime

Wonderland x Showtime is a theater group that works at a Theme Park and has a focus on musicals. This group started with Emu, who was able to start their dream with Tsukasa, Nene, and Rui. With the help of Wonderland Miku and KAITO, they are striving to achieve their dream of spreading smiles all over the world with their music through shows and musical songs.

Members: Emu, Tsukasa, Rui, and Nene. Vocaloids: Miku, Kaito.

let genre = "Musical Theater";

Nightcord at 25

Nightcord at 25 is a mysterious group that is only active at 25 o’clock, who’s genre is focused mainly on sad, baled type music with one or two wildcard songs. They are very secluded and only meet during online chat. With the help of Nightcord Miku, they hope to achieve their dream of saving people, and saving Mafuyu as well.

Members: Kanade, Mizuki, Ena, and Mafuyu. Vocaloid: Miku.

VIRTUAL SINGER

So you may have read the names such as Miku, Len, Rin, MEIKO, KAITO, and Luka; they are called Virtual Singers! They exist only in Sekai but can occasionally step out via phone like a hologram. They are there to help with each Units dream and goals. All of them are very unique with their Sekai to help them out.

They exist to support and mentor the characters in achieving their dreams.
